Tap water (highly advanced recipe)

Description

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • A glass (preferably heat-resistant)
  • Fire
  • Ice (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill glass with ice.
  2. Make fire.
  3. Hold glass over fire.
  4. ...
  5. Well done! You've made water!
  6. Add ice (optional).

Pro tip: Store any leftover water in the freezer. Keeps well for 12-14 business days.
